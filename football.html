Football Training

What will we work on?

Our football exercises focus on three main components: speed, strength, and explosiveness. All three parts coincide with each other and work in tandem. We will dive into four exercises per part.

Speed

As we've seen with the Miami Dolphins this season, speed kills. Speed is a crucial part to all positions of the sport, but especially to skill position and secondary players. We will train the trait through plyometrics, cardio, calistenics, and weights.

Reactive Acceleration

Depth Jumps + Broad Jumps

Hill Sprints

Unresisted Sprints

Strength

Strength is a skill that all football players need to have. You're never going to pancake an edge rusher, press up on a wide out, tackle a running back, or break a tackle without the proper strength to do so. We will be doing a lot of weight training, with some added calistenic work.

Hack Squat

Bench Press

Dips

Box Squats

Explosiveness

Explosiveness is the single best skill to have in the game of football. Every position on the field benefits from explosiveness, and it can be easily trained. Through weights, calistenics, and more, your explosiveness will blow through the roof.

Single-Legged Explosive Leg Press

Depth Jumps + Broad Jumps

Explosive Bulgarian Split Squats

Resisted Sprints
